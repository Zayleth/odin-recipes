Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish composed mainly of vinegared rice (sumeshi) accompanied by other ingredients such as raw or cooked fish, seafood, vegetables and eggs. It is characterized by its aesthetic presentation and its fresh and delicate flavor. Generally, it is composed of rice, as a base, raw fish, seafood, vegetables, egg for the filling; Likewise, it includes dried edible seaweed and other ingredients to complement.

There are various presentations of sushi, among the most common are, Nigiri (rice molded in the shape of an oval covered with a slice of raw or cooked fish), Maki (rice rolls wrapped in nori seaweed with various fillings in the center), Temaki (Nori seaweed cones filled with rice and other ingredients.). Generally speaking, sushi is not only a delicious food, but also a Japanese cultural expression that reflects its respect for nature and the importance of harmony.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sushi rice.
  • 2 cups of water
  • Sugar, 3 tablespoons.
  • 75ml minagre for sushi.
  • 1 makisu (bamboo mat to roll sushi)
  • 4 sheets of seaweed paper (nori)
  • 1/2 cups sesame seeds
  • 100 grams of cream cheese
  • 1 seedless cucumber
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 4 shrimp peeled and cooked

Steps

  1. Wash the rice very well, until the water runs clear, let it drain, boil the rice with water over medium heat, then cover it; Let it boil over high heat for 2 minutes, then over medium heat for 5 minutes. and finally, at a minimum heat of 15 meters.
  2. Let it absorb the rest of the water.
  3. Try not to uncover the pot for better results.
  4. Remove the lid, cover with a cloth and let it cool for 10 minutes.
  5. Pour it into a refractory dish and move it constantly, adding the vinegar until it is cold, keeping it covered with plastic.
  6. Place the makisu, top with a sheet of nori covered with rice and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  7. Turn the sheet over, on the edge place a thin layer of cream cheese, a strip of cucumber, a strip of avocado and shrimp.
  8. Roll and press with the makisu until the rolls are formed. Follow the same procedure with the other seaweed.
  9. Dampen a knife with water, cut the rolls into 6 or 8.
  10. Serve it accompanied by soy sauce with lemon and orange juice.
  11. NOTE:Also with this recipe, combine it instead of shrimp, make it with salmon, crab, tuna.
